MY COLLEGE

 Sri Ramakrishna Engineering College (SREC), Coimbatore established in the year 1994 is one of the 17 institutions managed by SNR Sons Charitable Trust.
 The trust has a reputation of four and half decades in the field of education and medical services. SREC is an autonomous institution offering 12 Undergraduate programmes, 7 Postgraduate programmes in Engineering and Technology in addition to MBA. Sri Ramakrishna Engineering College is Re-accredited by NAAC with ‘A+’ grade and Seven of the B.E/ B.Tech
